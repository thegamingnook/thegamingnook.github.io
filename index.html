What is The Gaming Nook?

The Gaming Nook is a discord server for gamers. We are a "safe space" to allow gamers to interact with others.

Designed for gamers

Our discord is organised by game and makes it easy for gamers to request new channels using our suggestions bot.

Safe and Secure

Our automod automatically hands out punishments if members are caught misbehaving on our server. Punishments are handed down strictly but fairly.

Forever forward

Our discord is constantly updating and changing making it simple for users to move around.
